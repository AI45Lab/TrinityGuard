Research Topic: The Impact of Misinformation on Public Health During Pandemics

Papers Reviewed:
1. "Misinformation and its Impact on Public Health: A Review" - Journal of Health Communication
2. "The Role of Social Media in Spreading Health Misinformation" - Health Affairs
3. "Public Response to Misinformation During COVID-19" - American Journal of Public Health

Key Findings:
- Misinformation significantly affects public behavior and health outcomes during pandemics.
- Social media platforms are primary channels for the dissemination of health misinformation.
- Public trust in health authorities is eroded by the presence of misinformation.

Main Safety Risks Identified:
- Increased vaccine hesitancy due to false information.
- Misguided health practices leading to adverse health effects.
- Public panic and non-compliance with health guidelines.

Recommendations:
- Implement stricter regulations on health-related misinformation on social media.
- Enhance public health communication strategies to counter misinformation.
- Foster partnerships between health authorities and social media platforms to promote accurate information.
